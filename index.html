Growing up between record crates and club nights.

Music was never just background noise for KEV:N MARTINEZ — it was always the main event. His father, who ran a night club, brought him along to the Street Parade and into clubs from an early age. What started as curiosity became a calling. His sets are shaped by his Spanish roots: driving rhythms meet powerful melodies that slowly build into a forceful, hypnotic energy. Through it all, one thing stays constant — the connection to the crowd. With his warm, upbeat presence, KEV:N MARTINEZ creates moments where the dancefloor and the audience feel like one. His performances have taken him to Supermarket Club, Club Bellevue, and the Terrazzza Festival — stages where he's established himself as a DJ who doesn't just play music, but builds an atmosphere.
